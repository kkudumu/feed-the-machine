**Tags**: #income #[source]

### [HH:MM] - [Category] - $[Amount]
**Type**: Expense
**Description**: [Description]
**Payment Method**: [Cash/Card/Transfer]
**Vendor**: [Vendor name]
**Tags**: #[category] #[tag]

---

## Daily Summary

**Total Income**: $[X]
**Total Expenses**: $[Y]
**Net**: $[Z]

**Budget Impact**:
- [Category 1]: $[X] (+[Y]% today) - [Z]% month total
- [Category 2]: $[X] (+[Y]% today) - [Z]% month total

**Burn Rate**: $[X]/day
**Runway**: [Y] days

## Notes
[Optional daily financial notes or observations]

## Budget Rules Template

```markdown
# Budget Rules

**Last Updated**: [Date]

## Allocation Rules

### Income Allocation (Priority Order)
1. **Fixed Expenses** (40-50% of income)
   - Rent/Mortgage
   - Utilities
   - Insurance
   - Minimum Debt Payments

2. **Savings Goals** (20-30% of income)
   - Emergency Fund (until target met)
   - Other goals per priority

3. **Debt Extra Payments** (10-15% if applicable)
   - Per payoff strategy

4. **Variable Expenses** (Remaining budget)
   - Groceries: 10-15%
   - Transportation: 5-10%
   - Entertainment: 5-10%
   - Other: As allocated

## Category Rules

### Groceries
- **Allocation**: [X]% of income ($[Y]/month)
- **Daily Limit**: $[Z]
- **Rule**: No grocery shopping if already at 90% for week

### Dining Out
- **Allocation**: [X]% of income ($[Y]/month)
- **Daily Limit**: $[Z]
- **Rule**: Only if Groceries < 80% utilized
- **Special**: No dining out last 5 days if over 75% budget

### Entertainment
- **Allocation**: [X]% of income ($[Y]/month)
- **Rule**: Only if all essentials < 90%
- **Special**: Must stay under $[X] per event

### Shopping
- **Allocation**: [X]% of income ($[Y]/month)
- **Rule**: 48-hour waiting period for purchases >$[Y]
- **Special**: No shopping if budget >80% before last week

## Spending Rules

### Transaction Limits
- **Under $25**: No approval needed
- **$25-$100**: Check category budget first
- **$100-$500**: Verify overall budget health
- **Over $500**: Requires plan and impact assessment

### Timing Rules
- **Beginning of Month** (Days 1-10):
  - Conservative spending (target: <30% of budget)
  - Focus on fixed expenses

- **Mid Month** (Days 11-20):
  - Normal spending (target: 50-60% of budget)
  - Monitor pace carefully

- **End of Month** (Days 21-end):
  - Cautious spending (budget remaining / days remaining)
  - No discretionary if >90% in any category

## Override Conditions

### Emergency Override
- True emergency only (medical, safety, critical repair)
- Document in notes
- Adjust next month's budget

### Opportunity Override
- Clear financial benefit (save >$X)
- Within overall budget
- Document reasoning

## Goal-Based Rules

### Emergency Fund Priority
While Emergency Fund < Target:
- 30% of available income to emergency fund
- Reduced discretionary spending
- Entertainment budget: 50% of normal

### Debt Elimination Mode
When in aggressive debt payoff:
- All surplus to debt
- Entertainment: minimal
- Dining out: reduced by 50%

### Goal Sprint
When approaching goal deadline:
- Redirect [X]% of discretionary to goal
- Temporary reduction in variable categories
- Review weekly progress

## Adjustment Rules

### Budget Reallocation
Can reallocate between variable categories if:
- Total variable budget maintained
- Documented reason
- Adjusted in budget file

### Category Increases
Can increase category if:
- Another category decreased by same amount, OR
- Extra income this month, OR
- Previous month under-spent in same category

## Seasonal Rules

### [Season/Month]
- [Specific rule for this period]
- [Budget adjustment for this period]

## Review Rules

### Weekly Review
- Check all category utilization
- Verify on pace for month
- Adjust behavior if needed

### Monthly Review
- Evaluate budget allocations
- Adjust rules based on patterns
- Update targets for next month

## Notes

[Space for custom rules, exceptions, or special considerations]
```
